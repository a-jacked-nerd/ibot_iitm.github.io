About iBot Club

iBot Club is the official robotics club of IIT Madras -> a playground for makers, coders, and dreamers.
We're a student-powered community building everything from AI-driven bots to bio-interfacing arms.

What makes us unique?
Hands-on learning, wild ideas, and a spirit of collaboration.
Whether you’re hacking your first bot, exploring computer vision, or leading a team at Robocon or Inter-IIT Tech Meet, you’ll find your tribe here.

We host workshops, innovation sprints, and showcase at Shaastra and Open House.
If you want to build, learn, and push the boundaries of robotics, this is your launchpad.
